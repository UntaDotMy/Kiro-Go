Why Kiro-Go

Built to feel native

Everything a production gateway needs — load balancing, failover, quotas, and observability — without slowing your requests down.

Near-native speed

HTTP/2 keep-alive pools to the upstream, lock-free token fast-path, and zero per-request waste — routing adds well under a millisecond.

Smart load balancing

Least-request routing with per-account adaptive concurrency steers traffic to the least-busy identity and absorbs throttling as smooth backpressure.

Transparent failover

An unlucky throttle on one account silently retries on a healthy peer before the first byte — your client never sees the 429.

Per-key quotas

Issue keys with daily, burst, lifetime and expiry limits, per-model whitelists, and timezone-aware resets. Sell access with confidence.

Live analytics

Per-model and per-key usage, reasoning-effort breakdowns, and realtime dashboards over WebSocket — all persisted across restarts.

Hardened for VPS

Scoped CORS, CSP and security headers, brute-force lockout, constant-time auth, and a customer portal that never leaks a key.
